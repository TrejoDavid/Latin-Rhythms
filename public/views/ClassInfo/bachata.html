Basic 1 Bachata

This class will teach you the four-beat basic step and movements of beginning Bachata, as well as:
  • Basic Bachata step & turns
  • Partnering techniques
  • Giving/receiving basic turn combinations
  • Footwork styling options
  • Music Theory

At the end of week 4, with this class and a little dedication, you'll be ready for Basic 2 Bachata!

Basic 2 Bachata

This class will teach you the essance of body movement in Bachata:
  • Fancier footwork patterns
  • Proper hip & rib movement
  • Introduction to body isolations
  • Half rotations
  • Complex wrap and lead reverse turn combinations

This class will prepare you for Basic 3 Bachata!

Basic 3 Bachata

This class will teach you the variations of Bachata Footwork:
  • Bachata cross body leads
  • Intricate footwork patterns
  • Intricate body isolations
  • Turn combinations incorporating body movement
  • Forward body rolls

This class will prepare you for Intermediate 1 Bachata!
