History

Latin Rhythms Dance Company was founded in January 2000, with a focus to promote the appreciation, spirit, and joy of Latin dance throughout Illinois. That same year, Ritmo Magazine named Latin Rhythms, “2000 Dance Company of the Year."

Through the founders’ direction, the company has recruited members who share their passion and love for dance. Each of LRDC’s dancers has a unique and exciting style, which they have developed over years of dancing. Their varied dance experiences include professional training, performance experience, and nightclub influence.

Adding to the distinctiveness of Latin Rhythms Dance Company, the dancers come from various professional and educational backgrounds, which range from bankers, to entrepreneurs, to law school students.
