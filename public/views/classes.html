Studio Policy

All paid student registrations are considered final. However, we realize life may throw us some curve balls, creating unforeseen circumstances that may force us/you to cancel or miss a class/session, (with prior notice). To accommodate these “curveballs,” we have established the following policies:

  • Make Up Policy: We strongly encourage you not to miss any of the four classes that make up your session. You’re paying for four classes, so it’s financially worthwhile for you not to miss.

    But more importantly, new material is covered every class, so missing a class would more than likely force you to fall behind. However, we realize unforeseen scheduling conflicts do arise, and therefore, you may be forced to miss a class.

    If this is the case, we encourage you to attend another class (same level/style) during the week the class was missed. Prior to this make-up class, you must notify your instructor or the front desk, in order to determine make-up class availability.

  • Refunds/Credits: Full refund or credit valid up to 3 months will only be issued if you contact Latin Rhythms to cancel your session prior to the start date of the first class.

    In the event a class is cancelled, you will have the option to request a full refund or receive a credit (in the amount paid), to be applied towards another running class during the current or the following 3 sessions. All refunds MUST BE requested prior to the next session start date. In the event a credit is requested, these credits will be good for 90 days only.

  • By enrolling in Latin Rhythms' Dance Studio, you agree that Latin Rhythms Dance Studio is NOT responsible for accidental injury or loss of property.
